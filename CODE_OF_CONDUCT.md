# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Project participants are expected to communicate professionally, review technical claims on their merits, protect sensitive information, and respect privacy and authorization boundaries.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, publication of private information, deliberate introduction of unsafe collection behavior, or encouraging unauthorized access.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and may restrict participation when conduct threatens project safety, legality, or professional collaboration.
